Burger Recipe

A satisfying veg burger featuring a flavorful veggie patty tucked between soft buns with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and zesty pickles—perfect for a quick and delicious meal !!!.

Preparation-time

  • Total: Approximately 18-20 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 8-10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Burger buns (whole wheat or regular)
  • Veggie patties (store-bought or homemade)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for cooking
  • Optional: sliced cheese

Instructions

1.

Prepare Veggie Patties: If making homemade patties, mash boiled potatoes and mix with finely chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, corn) or use a vegetarian meat substitute. Season with salt, pepper, and spices of your choice. Form into patties and pan-fry until golden brown.

2.

Toast the Burger Buns:Slice the burger buns in half and toast them lightly on a skillet or in the oven until golden.

3.

Assemble the Burger: Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of the bun and mustard on the top half. Layer lettuce leaves on the bottom bun. Place a cooked veggie patty on top of the lettuce. Add sliced tomatoes and onions on top of the patty. If using cheese, place a slice on top of the veggies.

4.

Add Condiments: Drizzle ketchup over the cheese or directly on the veggies.

5.

Top with Bun: Place the top half of the bun over the fillings to complete the burger.

6.

Serve:Your veg burger is ready to be served! Enjoy it with some extra ketchup or your favorite side dishes..
