Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick disk, perfect for any meal,. This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation-time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional: Fillings such as cheese, diced vegetables, or cooked meats

Instructions

1.

Whisk Eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together until well-beaten. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.

2.

Preheat Pan:Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add butter or oil and let it melt, ensuring it coats the bottom of the pan.

3.

Pour Eggs into Pan: Pour the beaten eggs into the heated pan, swirling it around to spread evenly.

4.

Add Fillings: Once the edges of the eggs begin to set, add your desired fillings (cheese, vegetables, or meats) to one half of the omelette.

5.

Fold and Serve: When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, carefully fold the omelette in half using a spatula. Cook for a little longer if needed, then slide it onto a plate.
