Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick disk, perfect for any meal,. This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation-time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 3-5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Optional: seasoning such as paprika, garlic powder, or mixed herbs

Instructions

1.

Prepare Potatoes: Wash and peel 4 large potatoes. Cut them into long, thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick.

2.

Soak in Water:Soak the potato strips in cold water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch.

3.

Drain and Dry: After soaking, drain the potato strips and pat them dry thoroughly using a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.

4.

Preheat Oil:Preheat your deep fryer with vegetable oil to around 350°F (175°C).

5.

Fry the Chips:Spread the potato strips in a single layer in the fryer basket, ensuring they're not overcrowded. Cook the potato strips in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes per batch. Remove the fried finger chips from the fryer and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

6.

Season and Serve:Season the fried finger chips with salt immediately after removing them from the fryer.Optionally, sprinkle with any other seasoning of your choice.Serve hot and crispy with your favorite dipping sauce.
